KitsuCore Privacy Policy, V.1.4, Updated on August 23rd, 2023.

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit, use, or create content with any Kitsu Website.

Personal information collected

When you visit a Kitsu site, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, as well as any cookies related to the website you are currently visiting. Additionally, as you browse the website, we collect information about the individual web pages that you view. We refer to this as "Device information".

We collect Device information using the following technologies:

  • Cookies are minuscule data files that are placed on your device or computer and often include an anonymous identifier. For more information about cookies, and how to disable them, visit AllAboutCookies.org.
  • Log files track pages that you access, and collect data including your IP address, browser type, ISP, and date/timestamps.

We also collect information that you explicitly give to us, such as posts, messages, photos, video, audio, or other 'user-generated content'.
Kitsu does not use any tracking technology beyond counters for read announcements.

How do we use your information?

We use this information to:

  • Communicate with you
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations, to respond to a subpoena, search warrant, or other lawful request, or to protect our rights
  • To enforce the rules and regulations of our, and other users's servers
  • Send content that you create to other servers through 'federation'

Who do we share information with?

  • Any other server who interfaces with the Kitsu Services will receive public information, i.e. any user content, or content you make available.
  • Kitsu does not share personal information, like your email, to other servers and we do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal identifiable information.
  • Photos, Videos, audio, media, or other non-text content will be submitted to Wasabi (https://wasabi.com/legal/privacy-policy) for long-term storage, and allow Fastly (https://www.fastly.com/privacy/) to cache this content.

Data retention

When you register for a Kitsu Service, we will maintain your information for both our records, unless and until you ask us to delete this information. Some information may be retained indefinitely in some circumstances; specifically, the emails/IP addresses of users who have been barred from our services.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to our practices or for other operation, legal, or regulatory reasons. We require all users to accept the new policies before allowing normal usage of the platform.

Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have any questions, or if you'd like to make a complaint, please contact us by email at kio@kitsunes.club.
